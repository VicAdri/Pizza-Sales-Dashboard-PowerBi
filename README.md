# 🍕 Pizza Sales Performance Report (2015)

# Executive Summary
This report analyzes a comprehensive sales dataset from a pizza establishment over the course of 2015. The dataset tracks precise order timestamps, pizza types, sizes, quantities, pricing, and ingredients. The goal of this analysis is to identify operational peak hours, customer buying habits, seasonality, and product performance to optimize menu offerings and marketing strategies.

# 🔍 Key Findings & Answers to Business Questions
## 1. Customer Traffic & Peak Hours
How many customers do we have each day? Are there any peak hours?
 - Daily Volatility: The restaurant averages 60 orders per day. Sales steadily build throughout the week, peaking on Friday (3,538 total orders), while Sunday experiences the lowest volume (2,624 total orders).
 - Hourly Peaks: Demand is heavily concentrated between 12:00 PM and 3:00 PM (6,446 orders), dropping slightly but remaining robust through the early evening from 6:00 PM to 9:00 PM (6,050 orders).

## 2. Consumer Behavior & Bestsellers
How many pizzas are typically in an order? Do we have any bestsellers?
 - Order Size: Customers typically purchase an average of 2 pizzas per order.
 - Top Performers: The Chicken category drives the highest individual item revenues. The top three best-selling pizzas are:

The Thai Chicken Pizza ($43,434),
The Barbecue Chicken Pizza ($42,768),
The California Chicken Pizza ($41,410),

 - Size & Category Preferences: Large (L) is the most popular size, accounting for 36.4% of orders, closely followed by Medium (M) at 31.9%. The Classic category is the most frequently ordered type of pizza (29.0%), closely rivaled by Supreme and Veggie.

## 3. Revenue & Seasonality
How much money did we make this year? Can we identify any seasonality in the sales?

 - Annual Revenue: Total sales reached $817,860.
 - Seasonal Trends: Sales peaked sharply in July ($72,558), followed by a steady, significant decline moving into the autumn, hitting an annual low point in October ($64,028) before recovering briefly in November.
